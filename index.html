Aryn Beitz

To get in touch, please say hi at aryn@arynbeitz.com

Aryn Beitz is a multidisciplinary designer and creative director engaged in ongoing partnerships with organizations and clients in the arts and culture, fashion, and media sectors. Projects include book design, exhibition and environmental graphics design, visual identity design, and writing and storytelling.

Previous positions include director of design and publishing at Carnegie Museum of Art, designer at The Shed, designer at Walker Art Center, and digital content producer at Gensler. She has also worked as a contributing writer for various design publications and online platforms. Aryn holds an MFA in communications design from Pratt Institute in New York.
